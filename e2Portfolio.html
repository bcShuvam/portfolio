Personal Statement
Hello, to the respective bodies. I am Shuvam BC excited to be applying for the BSc (Hons) Computer Science program at Biratnagar International College in association with the University of Wolverhampton through ING Group. Two major grounds for my excitement in computer science are my academic background and interest in the potential of technology in bringing about change. C was the first programming language I was taught while in high school at Shiksha Deep Boarding Secondary School, and it helped me get interested in coding. Out of class, I learned the basics of Python and JavaScript using internet materials such as YouTube tutorials, keeping my interest hot in using technology to build creative solutions. I have selected Biratnagar International College because it provides me with the opportunity to be locally based and yet receive a UK degree from an international campus. The most impressive aspect of this course to me is the strong practical learning component coupled with academic competence at the University of Wolverhampton. All together, they provide the best foundation for success in such exciting subjects as cloud computing, big data, and artificial intelligence. The institute boasts excellent facilities that will make it a great place to develop knowledge and skills, also providing a comfortable learning environment. I want to focus on full-stack software development, which aligns with this program's use of advanced technologies. I want to solve some of the most urgent problems and provide meaningful solutions for Nepal and globally. I would like to work overseas following the degree so that I can use my expertise to help innovative initiatives and continue learning more about the fast-evolving sector of technology. Once I have been accepted to Biratnagar International College and equipped with the knowledge and skills needed to become a computer professional, my life course will definitely change. Thank you for considering my application.
